# Cinemas

The script

1. Gets most recent films from Afisha
2. Fetches their ratings and amount of reviews from Kinopoisk
3. Sorts films by rating
4. Outputs 10 top-rated films with their names, ratings and amount of reviews.

Launch:

    $ python cinemas.py
